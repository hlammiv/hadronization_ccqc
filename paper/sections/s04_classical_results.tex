\subsection{Static validation and the correlated gas}

\begin{figure}[t]
\includegraphics[width=\columnwidth]{figs/fig2_static.pdf}
\caption{Static diquark-formation probability
[Eq.~\eqref{eq:pstatic}, hard wall, $k=\sqrt2$] versus per-species
density: Lebed's counting ($n_1{=}3n_2$) and the discrete-color counting
($n_1{=}2n_2$), analytic curves with ideal-gas Monte Carlo points; crosses
are Table~2 of Ref.~\cite{Lebed:2016jvq}.  The countings coincide in the
dilute (purely geometric) limit, cross at intermediate density, and
saturate at $51.5\%$ vs $41.4\%$.}
\label{fig:static}
\end{figure}

Our Lebed-mode Monte Carlo reproduces the published hard-wall value at
reference density, $50.45\pm0.12\%$ vs $50.29\%$, and
Fig.~\ref{fig:static} extends the comparison across five decades of
