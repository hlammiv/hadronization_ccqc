\section{Equal-time energy-momentum tensor normalization}
\label{app:emt}

\subsection{Ward-identity normalization (primary route)}

On the lattice the translation Ward identities are broken by $O(a)$
effects and $T^{\mu\nu}$ mixes with lower-dimensional operators; in the
Euclidean theory the finite renormalizations are fixed by
shifted-boundary or gradient-flow methods
\cite{Suzuki:2013gza,Makino:2014taa,DallaBrida:2020gux}.  Our
measurement is instead the \emph{equal-time} operator
$P^\mu_R=\int_R T^{0\mu}$ in the Hamiltonian theory, for which we use
the two exact handles the real-time setup provides:
(i) $H$ itself is the generator of the simulated evolution, so the
total energy of the prepared state is known by construction, and
(ii) total momentum is fixed by the state-preparation kinematics
(zero for the fireball; the flow kick adds a computable $P^i$).
Writing the renormalized charges as
$T^{0\mu}_{\rm ren}=Z_{\mu}\,T^{0\mu}_{\rm lat}+c_\mu\,\mathbb 1 a^{-3}
+\dots$, the sum rules
\begin{equation}
\sum_i P^\mu_{R_i} + P^\mu_{\rm vac-subtracted\ remainder}
= P^\mu_{\rm total}
\end{equation}
across the measured shots, applied at two or more fireball energies and
boosts, fix $Z_0$, $Z_i$, and the subtraction constants
nonperturbatively, with no flow and no new Euclidean computation.
Residual $O(a)$ boundary ambiguities of the region sums are absorbed
into the same window-smearing error budget as the charges
(Appendix~\ref{app:charge-quantization}).

\subsection{Division of labor and the flow outlook}

Species identification never relies on this route: Tier~2 masses come
from spectra (Appendix~\ref{app:spectral-projectors}).  Route~T supplies
per-cluster \emph{momenta} for Tier-3 kinematics, where normalization
errors enter only through smooth acceptance-like distortions of pair
spectra and cancel to first order in yield \emph{ratios} extracted with
a common kernel.  The systematic alternative --- a 3D small-flow-time
expansion on the time slice, i.e.\ the equal-time analog of
Refs.~\cite{Suzuki:2013gza,Makino:2014taa} --- requires new matching
coefficients and is deferred to a companion study.
